Sloppy Joes Recipe

Description

A sloppy joe is a sandwich consisting of ground beef, onions, tomato sauce or ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, and other seasonings, served on a hamburger bun. This is a recipe used for sloppy joes and it always gets compliments!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon prepared yellow mustard
  • ¾ cup ketchup
  • 3 teaspoons brown sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium skillet over medium heat, brown the ground beef, onion, and green pepper
  2. Drain off liquids
  3. Stir in the garlic powder, mustard, ketchup, and brown sugar; mix thoroughly.
  4. Reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
